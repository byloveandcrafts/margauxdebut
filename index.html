Deep Boho Hawaiian · Pink & Silver

Embrace the spirit of the islands with boho-chic elegance. Think flowy fabrics, tropical florals, soft blush tones, and shimmering silver accents. Adorn yourself in the colors of hibiscus and moonlit waves — and come ready to bloom alongside Margaux.

  • Formal to semi-formal attire preferred

  • Gentlemen — barong, linen suits, or tropical print shirts
  • Stick to: pink, silver, blush, gold, or deep plum tones
  • Hawaiian & tropical florals are warmly welcome
  • Boho accessories encouraged — shells, woven bags, floral crowns
